Publish company news with precision targeting and access control.

## Overview

The News module lets you create, schedule, and target announcements to specific departments, locations, or roles within your organization.

## Key capabilities

- **Targeted publishing** — Send news to specific departments, locations, or groups
- **Scheduling** — Set publication and expiration dates
- **Pinning** — Pin important articles to the top of feeds
- **Categories and tags** — Organize content for easy discovery
- **Access control** — Restrict who can see specific articles

## Creating a news article

1. Navigate to **Content → Add content → News**
2. Fill in the title, body, and featured image
3. Set targeting options (departments, groups)
4. Configure scheduling if needed
5. Publish
