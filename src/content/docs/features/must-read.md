Mark any content as mandatory reading and track compliance across your organization.

## Overview

Must-Read Tracking ensures critical information reaches every employee with proof of delivery — essential for safety procedures, policy changes, and compliance requirements.

## Key capabilities

- **Mark content as must-read** for specific users, groups, or departments
- **Automated reminders** sent to users who haven't read the content
- **Deadlines** with configurable timeframes
- **Compliance reports** showing who read what and when
- **Audit trail** for regulatory requirements

## Setting up must-read content

1. Create or edit any content item (news, knowledge base article, etc.)
2. Enable the **Must-Read** toggle
3. Select target audience (all users, specific groups, or departments)
4. Set a deadline (optional)
5. Save

The system will automatically track reads and send reminders.

## Reporting

Navigate to **Administration → Reports → Must-Read** to view compliance dashboards.
