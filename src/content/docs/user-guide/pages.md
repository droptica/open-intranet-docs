**Pages** are static content pages that provide information about the organization, departments, policies, and other resources that do not change frequently.

## Accessing pages

Pages are typically accessible through the main navigation menu under:

- **About Company** — General information about the organization, department pages, and legal documents.
- **Tools** — Forms, help requests, and other utilities.

Some pages are also linked from the footer under **Legal & Compliance** and **Quick Links**.

## Types of pages

### Department pages

Each department may have its own page with relevant information, contacts, and resources. These are accessible under **About Company > Department Pages** in the menu:

- HR
- IT
- Customer Support
- Marketing
- Finance

### Legal & compliance pages

The footer of every page links to important legal documents:

- **Code of Conduct**
- **GDPR & Data Protection**
- **Privacy Policy**
- **Terms of Use**

### Company information

General pages about the company, including the welcome page, are accessible through the navigation menu.

## Page features

Static pages can include:

- Formatted text with headings, lists, and emphasis
- Embedded images and media
- Links to other intranet pages or external resources
- File attachments for downloadable documents
