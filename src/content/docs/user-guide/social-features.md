Open Intranet includes social features that let you interact with content, save items for later, and engage with colleagues.

## Bookmarks

Bookmarks let you save any piece of content — news articles, events, Knowledge Base pages — for quick access later.

### Adding a bookmark

On any bookmarkable page, click the **Add to Bookmarks** button (or the bookmark icon). The item is added to your personal bookmarks list.

### Viewing your bookmarks

Access your bookmarks from the user menu:

1. Click your name in the top-right corner.
2. Select **My bookmarks**.

The bookmarks page shows all your saved items. You can click any title to navigate back to the content.

On the homepage, the **My Last Bookmarks** widget shows your most recent bookmarks for quick access.

## Comments

Many content types — including news articles — support comments. Commenting lets you share feedback, ask questions, or start discussions.

### Posting a comment

1. Scroll to the **Add new comment** section at the bottom of an article.
2. Type your comment in the text field.
3. Click **Save**.

Your comment appears immediately below the article, visible to all users.

## Reactions

The **like** feature lets you express appreciation for content without writing a comment:

- Click the **thumbs-up** (👍) icon below a news article.
- The like counter increments, showing how many people have reacted.

Reactions are a lightweight way to acknowledge content and help surface popular articles.
