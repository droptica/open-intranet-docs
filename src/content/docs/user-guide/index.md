This guide covers the features available to every employee using Open Intranet. Whether you need to read company news, find a colleague, browse documents, or submit a form, you will find step-by-step instructions here.

## Who is this guide for?

This guide is written for **authenticated users** — employees who log into the intranet with their account. It assumes no administrative privileges; administrators should also consult the [Administration](/docs/administration/users/) section.
